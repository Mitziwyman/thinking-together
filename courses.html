Time to Think Programmes

These courses are not about learning a technique. They are about experiencing something — a quality of attention that changes how you think, how you listen, and how you lead. The approach is Nancy Kline's Thinking Environment. The teaching is Mitzi Wyman's, drawn from many years of applying it across sectors and at the most senior levels of leadership.

Thinking Environment Foundation Course

Do you find yourself rushing from one meeting to the next, with barely time to draw breath — let alone think about the core and critical questions that really matter?

Do you long for time and space to think?

This two-day course introduces you to the Thinking Environment — Nancy Kline's approach to igniting the finest thinking in any gathering. You learn the Ten Components and the core building blocks: Thinking Pairs, Dialogue, Rounds and Open Discussion. You learn by being inside it, discovering the wisdom, compassion and courage that is available to you and those around you when people are truly listened to.

Thinking Partnership Course

Do you want time and space to think, free from the pressures of the everyday?

Do you want to liberate your own independent thinking — and create a deeply held space for others to do the same?

A deeply personal course, strictly limited to six participants. Over three days (in a 2+1 format) you will learn the Thinking Partnership Session — a uniquely powerful process for liberating the human mind. Through generative Attention and Incisive Questions, this practice produces breakthrough independent thinking, where people can say what they really think and others actually listen. The gap between the two days and the third creates space to bring your real experience back into the room.
