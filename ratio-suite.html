The Ratio Suite

Tools for making the invisible visible.

Most of the time, when a decision feels hard, it’s because two separate things have got tangled together. How strongly you feel about something, and how much it actually matters. How close you are to something, and how consequential it is. How much you’re intervening, and who needs to own it.

These ratios don’t untangle that for you. They just help you see it more clearly. Score each dimension honestly — the number doesn’t need to be precise, it needs to be true enough to examine. That’s where the thinking starts.

Before you pick a ratio

Bring a real situation. Something you’re carrying at the moment — a decision, a relationship, a question about where to spend your energy. These tools work on the specific, not the hypothetical.

For self-use and reflection. The moment you assign a number, you’ve committed to a position you can examine.
