A few minutes before the week begins

I don't know about you, but Sunday evening has a particular quality for me. The week is already present — not started, but felt. There's often a low hum of anxiety about what's coming, and most of us carry it quietly into Monday.

Fifteen minutes here changes that. Not by solving everything — by helping you see the week honestly before it takes over. What's really getting in the way. Where your attention actually needs to be. What would make it feel worthwhile.

Over time, something else starts to happen. You begin to notice patterns in how your weeks are shaped — and where there's room to shape them differently.
